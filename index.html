Ultrasound Experiment

I designed and assembled the electrical component of the first prototype of a device similar to the Myoband. Instead of using Electromyography electrodes, a nanoscale ultrasound transducer capable of transmitting and receiving a signal was used. Similar to an ultrasound probe a pulse echo would be transmitted and received. The signal received from the difference in depth between a resting and contracting muscle would be used for a computer interface.
